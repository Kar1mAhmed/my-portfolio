// selected projects

SceneYard sceneyard.com
backend architecture & build

Subscription SaaS for motion designers, with the backend designed and built solo. D1 is the relational store for users, subscriptions, and scene metadata, with a schema designed around subscription lifecycle state, and R2 handles large binary scene-file storage, chosen specifically to keep storage costs and cold-start latency low at the edge.

Billing runs on Paddle with a webhook-driven reconciliation layer that keeps subscription state in D1 in sync with Paddle's source of truth, and Retain handling automated dunning on failed payments. A background job system built on Cloudflare Cron Triggers reads and writes D1 to drive multi-step lifecycle email sequences through Resend, and a custom Telegram bot consumes internal events to give real-time visibility into signups, payments, and churn. Currently at 20,000+ signups.
